VULNERABILITY & RISK ASSESSMENT

We provide an in depth assessment on any risks or hindrances that may pertain to your area of travel. We will also provide information on the transportation system, logistics, areas to avoid, medical centers, and safe locations in the event of a civil disturbance/disaster.

INTELLIGENCE ANALYSIS

We provide custom-tailored verbal briefings to accommodate top-level corporate decision makers, government officials, diplomats, journalists, and others seeking in-depth and focused information and analysis. These briefings can be provided through Telephone/conference calls, webinar presentations, or held at a private location.
